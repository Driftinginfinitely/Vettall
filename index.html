Analysis & Depth

Prashna (Horary) Astrology casts a chart for the exact moment of a query. It is a profound system capable of analysing complex situations in great depth—from detailed relationship dynamics to business strategy. A Prashna chart zooms in on the specific mechanics of the present moment.

Pointed Answers

Because of its specificity, Prashna analysis offers pointed, clear-cut answers, and can often provide a definitive "Yes" or "No". It is best used for resolving specific dilemmas and obtaining actionable clarity on immediate questions.
