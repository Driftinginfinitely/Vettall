What is a Sigil?

A sigil is a symbol created to bypass the conscious mind and plant an intention deep in the subconscious. By turning a written intention (for example: "I HAVE STRENGTH") into an abstract glyph, you remove the language of doubt.

How to use:

  • Enter intention below (max 60 characters).
  • The generator removes vowels and repeated letters.
  • It creates a Crest (Left) and Star Path (Right).
  • Save the one that resonates with you.
